About Me

UI/UX Developer & Designer

I am Hanisha Deopurkar, a second-year Biomedical Engineering student at VIT with a strong interest in the intersection of healthcare and technology. My academic journey is complemented by hands-on experience in designing innovative medical devices that address real-world healthcare challenges. I have worked on multiple interdisciplinary projects, including an AI-powered Smart Medication Dispenser, a non-invasive heart health monitoring system, and an IoT-enabled portable oxygen concentrator for high-altitude environments. My approach blends biomedical knowledge with embedded systems, AI, and user-focused design. I am also an active member of BMESI and the FinTech Club's Creative Council, where I contribute to project ideation, content creation, and design. My strengths include creativity, attention to detail, and a strong foundation in medical technology and user-centric product development. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to making healthcare smarter and more accessible, I aim to pursue a career in biomedical innovation, medical product design, or healthcare technology management.
